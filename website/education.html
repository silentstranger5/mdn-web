On educational system

It somewhat plays across 'theory and application' but is more about educational system. Particularly high education in US. Let me introduce myself. I am from Russia. Yes, we had very strong academic world and very good education back in soviet times (or at least this is what elder people say). But the point is that now our education is just sucks terrible. Our educational system is absolutely awful. It really feels terrible. I literally had to go and learn English because I could not find anything in my native language. Guys, you are just insane. You really can not appreciate all of this free content which is available for you just for free. Oh, you now complain about tutorial hell, and stuff. But you guess what? I just had nothing, absolutely nothing to deal with. It is dead silence here, and many pieces of information are not represented in such form and quality and deep coverage in my language. Some automatic translation tools help, but that's not the solution.
