Home page

Honestly, I don't really feel like front-end developer. But power and ease with which you can build your web page is just amazing. Of course, this page is really simple, but genuinely I just like it. As well as process of creating such page. It is looking awesome for me. There are no bells and whistles, no gradients, round corners, no background images, or whatever. What I love the most is the fact that design process is absolutely and entirely controlled by me. I felt awful learning all of these concepts, that was really boring and dry. But that absolutely costed it. Not all things you can do are shiny and lovely out there. You need to be able to grind. This is really powerful when you can just sit for couple hours, and boom, your page is done. And I like it. And I like the process. It's fun.

Subsection

But raw html looks awful, and I really don't like that either. Yes, that took some time, but now I can do something just fine. My journey would probably continue to JavaScript, because that's just the most hireable language out there. (But entire market just collapsed, so all I can do now is just learning).
